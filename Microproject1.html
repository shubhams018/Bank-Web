Loans



  1. Eligibility:

    1. Must be between 21 years and 60 years of age

    2. For the self-employed: applicants may be required to have a specified minimum income, and financial documents that show stability of income.

    3. Must have had a job for at least 2 years, with a minimum of 1 year with the current employer.

    4. For an Smile Bank Salary Account holder: must have a minimum net monthly income of Rs 25,000.

    5. For a Non-Smile Bank Salary Account holder: must have a minimum net monthly income of Rs 50,000.

  2. Documents:

    1. Identity proof (copy of passport/voter ID card/driving license/Aadhaar)

    2. Address proof (copy of passport/voter ID card/driving license/Aadhaar)

    3. Bank statement for the previous 3 months (Passbook for previous 6 months)

    4. Two latest salary slips/current dated salary certificate with the latest Form 16

    5. Proof of end use

  3. Types of Loans:

    1. Education Loan
    2. Travel Loan
    3. Home Loan
    4. Emergancy Loan
    5. Health Loan

*Government taxes and other levies as applicable will be charged over and above the Fee and Charges. Loan disbursal is at the sole discretion of Smile Bank Ltd.

Note: The Procedure of loans can either be done in the bank or through home service(We will send our employee to check the documents.).
